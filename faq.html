FAQ's

1. What is Mentropy all about?
Mentropy is a platform that connects individuals seeking guidance with experienced mentors across various fields. We offer personalized, one-on-one mentorship sessions on an hourly basis to help you achieve your personal and professional goals.
2. What is Hourly Mentorship plan ?
Hourly mentorship is a flexible system where students receive one-on-one guidance from an experienced mentor for a set number of hours. This allows students to get personalized help on specific topics, tailored to their needs and schedules. It’s ideal for quick, focused support, especially for competitive exams like IIT/JEE, NEET, and NDA.
3. What is the Long-Term Mentorship Course?
Our long-term mentorship course provides continuous support and guidance over six months. Here are some key points:

1. What does it include?


Regular one-on-one sessions, detailed study plans, progress tracking, and personalized feedback.

2. Who is it for?


Students preparing for exams like IIT/JEE, NEET, and NDA need consistent support.

3. How often are the sessions?


Regularly scheduled, tailored to your study needs and exam timelines,.

4. What are the benefits of enrolling?


Ongoing mentorship, structured learning, timely doubt resolution, personalized strategies, and continuous motivation. This course is ideal for those seeking sustained and structured support for their exam preparation..
4. Who are the mentors ?
Our mentors are experienced professionals and top-performing students who have excelled in their respective fields and exams
5. How do I book a mentorship session?
You can book a session through our website by selecting the desired mentorship type (hourly or long-term), choosing a mentor, and scheduling a time that works for you.
6. What if I need to reschedule or cancel a session?
You can reschedule or cancel a session through your account on our website. We recommend making changes at least 24 hours in advance.
7. How do I track my progress?
For long-term mentorship, your mentor will provide regular progress reports and feedback to help you stay on track and improve continuously.
8. Is there a free trial available?
Yes, we offer a free introductory session for new students to experience our mentorship and decide if it's the right fit for them.
9. What are the payment options?
We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, online banking, and digital wallets.
10. What is the refund policy?
Our refund policy is designed to be fair and transparent. Please refer to the specific terms and conditions on our website for detailed information.
